Digital Prizm

Integrated Online System For SMEs

What is Digital Prizm?

Digital Prizm is an integrated SME guidance medium that guides SMEs to perform SME process up the class, ranging from doing Data Recording, Transaction Management and Business Process Management conducted offline to online.

Digital Prizm supports SME business management, among others:
- Recording Data and Transactions.
- Managing Business Processes Like Sales Process, Purchase Process and Inventory Process.
- Including Managing Employees and Making Financial Statements.
- With complete and flexible reporting and analysis features, SMEs can at any time make reports and perform the best analysis for their business development.

Digital Prizm also comes with a website maker and an online store that will make it easier for SMEs to start online marketing.

If you do not want to use this online store feature,Digtal Prizm integrates with multiple marketplaces so that SMBs can still manage their sales through the Punia Marketplace.

Despite its complete features, Digital Prizm can be used partially and gradually in accordance with the needs and capabilities of SMEs. This makes it easier for SMEs to apply technology according to their current business scale.

Digital Prizm has a complete and integrated operational support system. The existing features will make it easier for SMEs to monitor the operational and business development of their business online and on time.

Where in the end this will facilitate the making of operational and financial reports at the end of each period or at any time when necessary.
